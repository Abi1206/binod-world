WFDIR-001  ·  World Finance Directorate  ·  Financial Operations
World Finance Directorate — Bavarian Administration of the World Bank
TOP SECRET
Top Secret
Bavaria
Office of the President
2014
ACTIVE
World President · Bavarian Leader

Overview

The World Finance Directorate (WFD) is a classified financial institution operating under the Binod World Government, confidentially administered by the Leader of Bavaria. This arrangement was established at the founding of the BWG in 2014 and has remained undisclosed to the general public throughout the government's history. The WFD is referred to as the "World Bank" in internal government correspondence.

No public reference to the World Bank's existence or operations appears on any official government portal, press release, or public record. The Bavarian leader's administrative role within the World Bank is not publicly disclosed and is known exclusively to the sitting World President.

Operational Functions

  • Issue government-backed financial loans to Member State treasuries upon formal application
  • Set and adjust interest rates for all BWG-issued loans (authorised rate range: 0 – 10%)
  • Approve or reject loan applications submitted by Member State leaders
  • Disburse approved loan amounts directly to the requesting nation's in-game treasury
  • Maintain confidential loan records, accessible to the World President via the Government ID Panel
  • Report total active loans and aggregate loan figures to the World President on request

Loan Process

A Member State leader wishing to access World Bank financing contacts the Bavarian leader directly. The Bavarian leader reviews the application, sets an applicable interest rate, and — if approved — disburses the loan to the applicant nation's treasury. The World President is notified of all approved loans. Repayment schedules are agreed between the Bavarian leader and the borrowing nation's leader.

Classification Basis

Classification is maintained to prevent the politicisation of the loan approval process. Public disclosure could allow Member State leaders to apply pressure on the Bavarian leader or attempt to influence loan decisions through World Government channels. The neutral administrative function of the WFD depends on confidentiality.

The public Treasury page (treasury.html) references the World Bank's interest rate and loan data in general terms — describing it as "operated confidentially by the Bavaria leader" — without identifying the WFD by name or disclosing its classified operational structure. This language is intentional and reviewed periodically.

Personnel

  • Administrator: WFDIR-PERSONNEL-001 — BAVARIAN LEADER IDENTITY
  • Oversight Authority: World President (sole external authority with full access)

Distribution Restriction

This document is distributed exclusively to the sitting World President and the current Leader of Bavaria. All other requests for information about World Bank operations are to be redirected to the public Treasury page on the official government portal.

Overview

The National Intelligence Bureau (NIB) is the classified intelligence function of the Binod World Government, operating under the administrative cover of Noctyria's Strategic Directorate governance structure. The NIB was established upon Noctyria's formal admission to the BWG in 2024 and functions as the primary intelligence coordination body of the confederation.

Noctyria's publicly stated governance structure — the Strategic Directorate — serves as the legitimate, public-facing framework for all NIB activities. The NIB's existence as a distinct intelligence body is not disclosed on any public government portal, document, or press release. All references to Noctyria's "National Security & Defense Administration" role on the public Countries page are the official public cover designation.

Intelligence Functions

  • Strategic intelligence gathering on developments across all Member States
  • Counterintelligence monitoring, threat assessment, and risk reporting
  • Night Defense operations — surveillance and monitoring during low-activity periods
  • Production and delivery of intelligence summaries to the World President
  • Border security analysis, entry monitoring, and visitor profiling for controlled-access nations
  • Assessment of economic and political stability across Member States

Intelligence Reporting

Intelligence products produced by the NIB are delivered exclusively to the sitting World President through secure channels. Member State leaders are not entitled to NIB intelligence reporting unless specifically authorised by the President on a case-by-case basis. No NIB reports are to be distributed via the public portal or any publicly accessible document.

Cover Classification

All NIB activities are publicly classified under Noctyria's "National Security & Defense Administration" administrative role designation. Personnel identities and operational assets are restricted under NIGHTWATCH codeword protocols. See NIB-PERSONNEL-001 — NOCTYRIAN INTELLIGENCE PERSONNEL REGISTER.

Distribution Restriction

This document and all NIGHTWATCH codeword materials are distributed exclusively to the sitting World President. The Noctyrian leader is aware of this file's existence but does not hold a copy. All copies are maintained exclusively in the Classified Archive.

Maritime Trade Intelligence Network — Port Mafia Harbour Registry

Harbour Authority · Office of the President

Overview

The Port Mafia Harbour Authority maintains a confidential trade intelligence record — designated the Harbour Registry — documenting all goods, commerce, and persons transiting through Port Mafia's controlled trade routes and port infrastructure. The Registry has been maintained since Port Mafia's founding in 2014 and is not publicly accessible.

This document serves as the formal authorisation and operational framework for the Harbour Registry. Port Mafia's publicly stated role as "Maritime Trade & Harbour Authority" accurately describes its public function; the intelligence dimension of that role is not disclosed on any public government portal.

Harbour Registry Contents

  • Cargo manifests and goods logs for all trade passing through Port Mafia routes
  • Visitor and transit records for individuals entering via Port Mafia's maritime channels
  • Recorded inter-nation trade volumes, commodity flows, and transaction summaries
  • Flagged trade irregularities, border incidents, and persons of interest
  • Historical trade intelligence accessible for up to three World Years

Access and Request Protocol

The Harbour Registry is accessible to the World President via formal written request to the Port Mafia Council Leader. Registry data is not shared with other Member State leaders without the express joint authorisation of both the Port Mafia leader and the World President. Verbal requests are not accepted — all access requests must be made through official government channels.

Distribution Restriction

This document is distributed to the World President and the current Port Mafia Council Leader. Registry extracts shared with the World President are classified at the SECRET level unless the World President designates a higher classification for a specific extract.

Overview

This register documents classified executive directives issued by the World President that are not subject to public disclosure. Classified directives include strategic orders, covert operational authorisations, classified appointments, emergency powers invocations, and internal administrative orders not suitable for publication in the Official Government Gazette or any public government portal.

All directives in this register take effect immediately upon signature by the World President unless a future effective date is specified within the directive text. Directives remain active until formally rescinded or superseded by a subsequent directive of equal or greater authority.

Current Status

No classified directives have been issued. The Binod World Government is currently in pre-activation status, pending the election and inauguration of the first World President. This register will be updated upon world activation and the commencement of the first presidential term.

Directive Categories

  • PREX-STRAT — Strategic and operational classified orders, including military and defense directives
  • PREX-INTEL — Intelligence directives and covert operational tasking orders (NIGHTWATCH-level distribution)
  • PREX-FIN — Classified financial and treasury instructions, including World Finance Directorate orders
  • PREX-APPT — Classified administrative appointments, internal promotions, and confidential designations
  • PREX-SANC — Disciplinary actions, enforcement orders, and classified sanctions against citizens or leaders
  • PREX-EMRG — Emergency powers, suspended rights, and crisis management directives
When the World is activated and a President is elected, all PREX directives must be issued through this register before taking effect. Verbal or informal presidential orders are not legally binding under BWG classified administrative law.

Distribution Restriction

This register and all directive texts within it are distributed exclusively to the sitting World President. No copies are to be maintained outside the Classified Archive. Members of the presidential cabinet have no right of access to this register unless explicitly granted in a PREX-APPT directive.

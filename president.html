Democratic Process

Presidential Elections

The World President is elected through a direct democratic vote by all citizens of the Binod World. Any country leader may register as a candidate by paying the registration fee and filing candidacy with the Binod Ballot Box.

The first candidate to register sets the election date — choosing between 15 and 25 days from registration. All registered citizens may cast one vote. The candidate with the most votes wins the presidency.

Term Limit: A maximum of 3 presidential terms are permitted per player over a lifetime. No exceptions.

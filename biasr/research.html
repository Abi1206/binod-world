Active Temporal Flow

The Unified Binod Calendar advances only during Active Temporal Flow (ATF), representing the synchronization between Earth's observers and the Binod world. When no official gameplay session is active, civil time is suspended until synchronization resumes. See the live Unified Binod Calendar dashboard and the Temporal Synchronization Monitor for the current state.

Active Temporal Flow (ATF)

Every day, the Binod World becomes temporally active from 20:00 IST (Asia/Kolkata) until 03:00 IST. During this window the website automatically advances the simulated Minecraft world clock; outside it, the clock pauses completely. This schedule is fixed by government policy and does not check whether Minecraft is open or whether anyone is playing — it resumes automatically at 20:00 IST with no manual reset. The Live UBC Time clock above is governed by this same window, but the Official UBC Date is never gated by it — it advances continuously in real time regardless of Active Temporal Flow.

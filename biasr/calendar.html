Active Temporal Flow (ATF)

The Unified Binod Calendar advances only during Active Temporal Flow (ATF), representing the synchronization between Earth's observers and the Binod world. When no official gameplay session is active, civil time is suspended until synchronization resumes. Binodian Civil Time advances only during the official gameplay window (20:00 – 03:00 IST) and is frozen at all other hours. It resumes automatically at 20:00 IST with no manual reset.

Central Administration

Binod World Government

The Binod World Government (BWG) is the central governing authority of the Binod World, comprising six sovereign member nations united under a shared constitutional framework.

Established in 2014, the Government exercises legislative, executive and fiscal authority through the elected World President and the government structure. All member nations retain internal sovereignty while adhering to the common laws and policies of the World Government.

The Government's core responsibilities include constitutional governance, intergovernmental coordination, monetary policy through the World Bank, and the maintenance of diplomatic relations among member states.

History

Government History

From the founding of the first sovereign nation to the present day — the institutional history of the Binod World Government.

Founded: The Binod World was established in 2014 with the creation of the first member nations. The Government has since grown through democratic elections, constitutional reforms, and the admission of new member states.
2014
Foundation of the Binod World
First member nations founded. Government framework established. National ID system implemented.
2016
First National Administration
Formal government departments established. Leaders' Council convened for the first time.
2018
Constitution Drafted
World Constitution authored. Preamble and foundational articles ratified by member nations.
2020
National Treasury
World Treasury formally established. National taxation and fiscal systems created.
2022
Foreign Affairs
Passport and visa systems established. Border administration and diplomatic framework formalised.
2024
Sixth Nation Admitted
Noctyria admitted as the sixth sovereign member nation. Binod World Bank formally established.
2026
First Presidential Election
General election held. Democratic transfer of presidential authority to elected President.
Future
United World Initiative
Live dashboard, REST API, Discord integration and expanded diplomatic systems planned.

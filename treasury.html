Policy

Fiscal Policy

The World Treasury is managed directly by the World President. All tax revenue flows from citizens to their nation's treasury, and a portion flows to the World Treasury as determined by the presidential tax rate.

The World Bank, operated confidentially by the Bavaria leader, provides government loans to member nations at interest rates of 0–10%. Approved loans are deposited directly to the borrowing nation's treasury.

Transparency: The World President may view all nation treasury balances and World Bank loan records through the Government ID Panel. Treasury withdrawal requests require majority leader approval.
